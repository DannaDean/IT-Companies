    Meta Platforms, doing business as Meta and formerly named Facebook is an American multinational technology. Meta is one of the world's most valuable companies. It is considered one of the Big Five American information technology companies, alongside Alphabet, Amazon, Apple, and Microsoft.In October 2021, the parent company of Facebook changed its name from Facebook, to Meta Platforms, to "reflect its focus on building the metaverse". According to Meta, the "metaverse" refers to the integrated environment that links all of the company's products and services.
